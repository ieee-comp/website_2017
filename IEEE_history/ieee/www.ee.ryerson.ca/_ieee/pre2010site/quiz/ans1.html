QUESTION#1: TEN BASKETBALL PLAYERS
Difficulty: not-rated

ANSWER IS:

  1. The probability is 0. If nine players get their own shirt then the tenth is certain to get his.
  2. The probability that the first player gets his own shirt is 1/10. The probability for the rest is 1/9,1/8….1/2, so the probability that all the ten players get their own shirt is 1/10! Or 2.756E-7, still a pretty small number

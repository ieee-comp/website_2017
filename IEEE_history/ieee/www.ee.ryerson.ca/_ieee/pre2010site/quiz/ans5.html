THE IEEE ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING ONLINE QUIZ

QUESTION#5: THE THREE PIPED WATER TANK
Difficulty: not-rated

ANSWER IS:

In time t, pipe A lets in the fraction t/3 of a fill, pipe B lets in the fraction t/4 of a fill and pipe C drains t/7. This yields the equation,

• i. t/3 + t/4 – t/7 = 1

Solving for t yields the amount of time it takes which is 2.27 hours.

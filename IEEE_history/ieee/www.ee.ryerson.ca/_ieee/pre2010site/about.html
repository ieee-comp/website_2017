THE IEEE STUDENT BRANCH @ RYERSON UNIVERSITY
BACKGROUND INFORMATION


The IEEE student branch of Ryerson University was founded on January 2nd, 1963 by the electrical engineering students. The IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers) is the largest professional organization consisting of over 300,000 members. This extensive network consists of professionals within the field of electrical and electronic engineering. Over 40,000 members are students working towards degrees within the electrical and computer engineering field. This IEEE student branch was formed to serve the student body currently completing the Bachelor of Electrical Engineering degree at Ryerson University. Currently this branch is operated by an executive committee elected by IEEE student members. We provide members with the services listed in the "Services" section. Any new branch information will be listed in our "Events" section. The IEEE branch office is located in room ENG110 within the Engineering building on the Ryerson Campus.

Membership Information

Those who can qualify to have student membership in the IEEE must be a registered undergraduate or graduate student in a regular course of study in an IEEE designated field and must carry at least a 50% course load of a normal full-time academic program. Examples of these are the Electrical and Computer Engineering, Computer Science and Aerospace Engineering Programs here at Ryerson.

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE)

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is the world's largest technical society in terms of any other engineering discipline. The IEEE was formulated in 1884 where its name was originally Brain-Child.

IEEE membership benefits include career services, access to software, the latest news on publications & standards, magazine subscriptions & more.

For more info. and to join IEEE please visit: IEEE online

The Ryerson Student Branch

The Ryerson University IEEE Student Chapter was founded on January 2nd, 1963 by the electrical engineering students. Since then, the branch has served the student body currently completing the Bachelor of Electrical & Computer Engineering degree at Ryerson University.

This branch is operated by a group of undergraduate biomedical, computer, electrical, and mechanical engineering students with a passion for technology & all things engineering!
